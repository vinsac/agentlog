## Debt Reduction Strategy

### Prioritization Framework
- **Impact**: Business and technical impact
- **Frequency**: How often the debt causes issues
- **Cost**: Effort required to address
- **Risk**: Risk of not addressing the debt

### Reduction Approach
- **Incremental**: Address debt during regular development
- **Dedicated Sprints**: Focused debt reduction periods
- **Prevention**: Practices to avoid new debt
- **Monitoring**: Continuous debt tracking

## AI Agent Guidelines

- Consider technical debt in implementation decisions
- Avoid introducing new technical debt
- Flag potential debt issues during development
- Contribute to debt reduction when possible
