# Product Requirements Document Template

## Purpose

- Provides standardized template for product requirements
- Ensures comprehensive product planning and documentation
- Facilitates cross-team alignment and communication
- Guides AI agents in product-aligned development

## Template Structure

### Executive Summary
- **Product Vision**: [Long-term product vision]
- **Problem Statement**: [Core problem being solved]
- **Solution Overview**: [High-level solution approach]
- **Success Metrics**: [Key success indicators]

### Market Analysis
- **Target Market**: [Market size and characteristics]
- **User Personas**: [Primary user profiles]
- **Competitive Landscape**: [Competitor analysis]
- **Market Opportunity**: [Business opportunity]

### Product Requirements
- **Core Features**: [Essential functionality]
- **Secondary Features**: [Important but not critical]
- **Future Features**: [Planned enhancements]
- **Out of Scope**: [Explicitly excluded items]

### User Experience
- **User Journey**: [End-to-end user flow]
- **Interface Requirements**: [UI/UX specifications]
- **Accessibility**: [Accessibility requirements]
- **Internationalization**: [Globalization needs]

### Technical Requirements
- **Architecture**: [Technical architecture]
- **Performance**: [Performance requirements]
- **Security**: [Security specifications]
- **Scalability**: [Growth and scaling needs]

### Business Requirements
- **Business Model**: [Revenue and monetization]
- **Go-to-Market**: [Launch strategy]
- **Pricing**: [Pricing strategy]
- **Support**: [Customer support requirements]

### Implementation Plan
- **Timeline**: [Development milestones]
- **Resources**: [Team and resource requirements]
- **Dependencies**: [External dependencies]
- **Risks**: [Potential risks and mitigations]

### Success Metrics
- **KPIs**: [Key performance indicators]
- **User Metrics**: [User engagement metrics]
- **Business Metrics**: [Business success metrics]
- **Technical Metrics**: [System performance metrics]

## Usage Guidelines

1. Complete all sections relevant to your product
2. Use data-driven decision making
3. Review with all stakeholders
4. Update regularly as product evolves

## AI Agent Guidelines

- Reference PRD for all development decisions
- Ensure alignment with product vision
- Track progress against defined metrics
- Communicate deviations from requirements
